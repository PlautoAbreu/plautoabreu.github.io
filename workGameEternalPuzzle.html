ETERNAL PUZZLE

Minimalist iOS game. Here the player clicks on the screen to create circular shockwaves that travel the screen and destroy a set of targets. To destroy the targets, two shockwaves must intersect directly over them. In order to design the stages.

An automated stage designer tool was developed to speed up the level creation.

Date: November 2014 - Platform: iOS

ABOUT
