3D/2D MOBA MATCH-3 HYBRID

This is the result of a 2 Day game programming challenge proposed by Digit Gaming at the 2017 Vanhackathon.

The game proposed was a Hybrid of Moba with Match-3, where you use the match 3 grid to get energy to summon troops on the 1 lane Moba.

Date: April 2017 - Platform: PC/Mobile
