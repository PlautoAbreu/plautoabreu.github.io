THE CRANE

I Love Cranes ! This prototype is just the initial ideia for a crane controlling game. The ideia is to pick up and move cargo
from point A to point B, in the least amount of time.

The game will scale by increasing the amount cargo, by lowering the time to do it and by adding moving cargo/drop locations, the currency aquired will be expendable on crane upgrades.
